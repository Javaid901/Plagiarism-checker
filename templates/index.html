Analysis Lab

Each engine below performs deep structural, statistical, and linguistic analysis on your text.

AI Forensics Engine

Statistical deep-scan that analyzes burstiness, perplexity, repetition patterns, and AI-typical phrasing to determine if text was written by a human or generated by an LLM. Returns a confidence score with granular breakdown.

Plagiarism Intelligence

Sentence-level semantic matching engine that cross-references your text against indexed web sources. Detects verbatim copying, near-duplicates, and paraphrased content with similarity scoring for every sentence.

Neural Paraphraser

Rewrites your text at three intensity levels while preserving semantic meaning. Uses synonym substitution, syntactic restructuring, and clause rearrangement to produce natural-sounding variations.

Humanization Engine

Transforms stiff, robotic, or AI-generated text into authentic human writing. Introduces natural discourse markers, varied sentence rhythms, colloquial phrasing, and the subtle imperfections that make writing feel genuinely human.

Grammar Perfectionist

Comprehensive grammar, spelling, and style checker powered by rule-based and statistical models. Detects subject-verb agreement errors, punctuation misuse, awkward phrasing, and offers one-click corrections with before/after comparison.

AI Detection Bypass

Advanced adversarial transformation engine that restructures text to evade AI classifiers. Applies discourse marker insertion, hedging, sentence-length variance, colloquial substitution, and controlled imperfection to reduce AI detection scores significantly.

Bypass Strategies:

  • Discourse marker injection (“however,” “meanwhile”)
  • Hedging & tentative language
  • Extreme sentence-length variation
  • Colloquial & idiomatic substitution
  • Controlled grammatical imperfections
  • Before/after AI score comparison

Smart Document Formatter

Intelligently detects headings, bullet points, numbered lists, and paragraphs — or choose from specialized formatting options below.

Extractive Summarizer

Intelligent sentence-scoring engine that identifies the most important sentences using TF-based frequency analysis, positional weighting, and length normalization. Condenses any text to ~30% of its original length while preserving key meaning.

Writing DNA Analyzer

Deep linguistic profiling that measures four dimensions of your writing: formality vs. casualness, conciseness vs. wordiness, passive voice density, and vocabulary complexity. Includes filler word detection and readability estimation.
